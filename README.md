# 🔬 SciBridge AI
### Bridge Between Research and School Experiments

SciBridge AI is an interactive STEM learning platform designed for **school students from Grade 4 to Grade 12**.  

The platform converts **complex scientific concepts and research papers into simple, fun, and interactive experiments**, helping students understand science visually and practically.

SciBridge AI acts as a **bridge between scientific research and school-level experiments**.

---

# 🚀 Problem

Many students struggle with science because:

- Schools often lack proper laboratory facilities.
- Scientific research papers are difficult for school students to understand.
- Students find it hard to connect **theory with practical experiments**.

As a result, science learning becomes **boring and difficult**.

---

# 💡 Solution

SciBridge AI solves this problem by providing a **virtual AI-powered experiment platform** where students can:

- Learn science concepts in a simple way
- Perform virtual experiments
- Convert research papers into easy experiments
- Watch recommended learning videos
- Test knowledge through quizzes

The platform makes **science learning fun, visual, and interactive**.

---

# 🎯 Key Features

## 🔬 Explore Experiments
Students can explore science experiments from different subjects:

- Chemistry
- Physics
- Biology

Example experiments include:

- Acid Base Reaction
- Electrolysis of Water
- Magnetism
- Photosynthesis
- Volcano Reaction

Each experiment includes:
- Simple explanation
- Materials list
- Step-by-step process
- Interactive simulation

---

## 🧪 Virtual Lab Simulator

Students can perform experiments inside a **virtual science laboratory**.

Example interactions:

- Mix chemicals
- Add solutions
- Connect circuits
- Observe reactions

This allows students to safely perform experiments without a real lab.

---

## 📄 Research to Experiment

Students can paste **scientific research text**, and the platform will convert it into:

- Simple explanation
- Key concepts
- School-level experiment
- Materials
- Step-by-step procedure

---

## 📤 Upload Research Paper

Users can upload a research paper and SciBridge AI will:

- Summarize the paper
- Simplify the explanation
- Convert it into a **school-friendly experiment**

---

## 🤖 AI Science Assistant

Students can ask science questions and receive **simple explanations**.

Example questions:

- Why do acids react with bases?
- How does gravity work?
